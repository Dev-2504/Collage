------------- Chapter :- 2 -------------

1. _____ is any program or file that’s intentionally harmful to a computer.
-> Malware.

2. Define: Financial Fraud.
-> Financial fraud e paisa ya financial information ne cheat ya illegally use karvanu cyber crime che.

3. The attacker locks and encrypts victim’s data and demands money — it is known as ______ attack.
-> Ransomware attack.

4. What is Cyber Law?
-> Cyber law e internet ane digital activities ne control karva mate banaavela kanoon che.

5. What is Cyber Crime?
-> Cyber crime e koi pan aavi criminal activity che je computer ya internet dwara thay che.

6. Full form of IT Act _____.
-> Information Technology Act.

7. IT Act was implemented in which year?
-> Year 2000.

8. Define: Social Engineering Attack.
-> Social engineering e loko ne manipulate kari information ya password medvani trick che.

9. Define: Malware.
-> Malware e malicious software che je computer ne damage kare che.

10. Define: Phishing.
-> Phishing e fake email ya website thi user ni personal information medvani trick che.

11. Cyber crime against women include _____.
-> Cyber stalking, harassment, morphing, and cyber bullying.

12. Cyber crime targeting mobiles includes _____.
-> SMS fraud, mobile malware, SIM swapping.

13. Define: Zero-day attack.
-> Zero-day attack e software ma navi weakness find thai tyare immediately exploit karva ma aave te attack che.

14. Define: Zero-click attack.
-> Zero-click attack ma user ne koi click karvu na pade — attacker automatically exploit kare che.

15. What is Cybercriminal’s Modus Operandi?
-> Modus Operandi e cyber criminal kevi rite plan kari crime kare che te bataave che.

16. Define: Ransomware.
-> Ransomware e aavu malware che je user ni file encrypt kari ransom (money) mange che.

17. What is the main aim of Cyber Law?
-> Internet par illegal activities ne control karvu ane users ne protection aapvu.

18. Name one organisation dealing with cyber crime in India.
-> CERT-IN (Computer Emergency Response Team - India).

19. Cyber crime ni reporting kya thai che?
-> www.cybercrime.gov.in

20. What are remedial measures of cyber crime?
-> Strong passwords, antivirus use karvu, data backup rakhvu, ane awareness badhavu.
